Our Technical Services

SANS-aligned, high-precision gas installations and piping solutions across commercial, medical, laboratory, and industrial sectors nationwide.

Service 01

Laser Installations

We design and construct high-purity laser gas reticulation systems that meet strict industry specifications. Resonator and assist gases (nitrogen, oxygen, carbon dioxide, and helium) must remain completely free of moisture and particulate contamination to protect expensive laser cutting optics and ensure edge-cutting precision.

What's included:

  • High-purity gas manifold setups and dual-stage pressure regulators.
  • Stainless steel orbital-welded or compression-fitted piping lines.
  • Helium leak-testing, pressure purging, and full SAQCC Certificate of Compliance (SAQCC e-CoC) sign-off.

Service 02

Laboratory Gas Piping

Analytical instruments like gas chromatographs (GC), mass spectrometers (MS), and atomic absorption systems require stable pressures and ultra-high purity (UHP) gas supplies. We install laboratory-grade copper or stainless steel pipelines with proper purge assemblies and point-of-use gas panels.

What's included:

  • UHP gas reticulation (99.999% purity lines) with specialized purge valves.
  • Wall-mounted outlet panels, micro-metering regulators, and pressure sensors.
  • Gas leak verification and issuing of the SAQCC compliance certificate.

Service 03

Welding Gas Supply & Installation

For fabrication workshops and manufacturing plants, we install central welding gas manifolds and piping distribution. Centralizing gas cylinders outside the workshop increases floor safety, simplifies cylinder management, and provides a continuous flow of shield gases (argon, carbon dioxide mixtures, oxygen, and acetylene).

What's included:

  • Centralized gas cylinder manifold manifolds with safety flashback arrestors.
  • Heavy-duty piping layout to workbenches or welding bays.
  • Pressure testing, safety checks, and SANS-compliant SAQCC e-CoC certification.

Service 04

Medical Gas Piping

We execute highly regulated medical gas piping projects for hospitals, dental clinics, veterinary surgeries, and outpatient centers. All installations conform to stringent medical sanitary codes, ensuring leak-free delivery of oxygen, nitrous oxide, medical air, and vacuum lines.

What's included:

  • Installation of medical-grade copper pipelines, degreased for oxygen services.
  • Outlet boxes, medical gas manifolds, zone alarm valves, and emergency shut-offs.
  • Pressure decay testing, cross-connection checks, gas purity testing, and Digital SAQCC e-CoC issuance.

Service 05

Bulk Tanks & Reticulation

For industries requiring high volumes of gas, we coordinate bulk tank installations and piping reticulation networks. We calculate optimal line sizes, select vaporizers, install control blocks, and run underground or overhead distribution pipelines to primary process consumption zones.

What's included:
